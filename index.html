Brief Bio

Yulan Guo (Gary) is now working with Prof. Xilin Chen and Shiguang Shan as a postdoctoral research fellow at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. He is also an assistant professor at NUDT and an adjunct research fellow at the University of Western Australia (UWA). He received his PhD degree from NUDT in 2015. His research interests include computer vision and pattern recognition, particularly on 3D feature extraction, 3D modeling, 3D object recognition, and 3D face recognition. He is also interested in the applications of 3D comuputer vision theories in robotics, remote sensing, intelligent transportation, and ubiquitous computing.
